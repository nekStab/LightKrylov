Algorithmic Features

  • Only diagonal approximations ( ) are used as these are more efficient to compute than off-diagonal approximations for the same approximation order
  • 12th order diagonal Pade approximation by default

Advantages

  • Very accurate for "small" matrices (in terms of their norm). When considering the temporal evolution of an LTI system, i.e. , the approximation is very good for small .
  • Avoids numerical problems linked to the "hump" appearing for non-normal matrices

Limitations

  • No error estimation/check based on the approximation order and the matrix norm

expm is an adaptation of the function R8mat_expm1 by C. Moler & C. van Loan.
